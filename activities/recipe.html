Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled egg is a very simple food you can make at home under 10 minutes.

List of ingredients

  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Salt & pepper

Steps to follow:

  1. Mix egg and milk then stir well
  2. Put them in a pan then start stirring until it becomes scrambled
  3. Add salt and pepper to your taste and enjoy
